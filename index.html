About Dengue Fever

Dengue fever is a mosquito-borne viral infection that causes flu-like symptoms and can be fatal.

It is a major public health concern in many parts of the world, including Lao PDR.

Preventive measures such as eliminating mosquito breeding sites and using insect repellent are crucial in controlling the spread of dengue.

Together, we can make a difference in the fight against dengue fever.
